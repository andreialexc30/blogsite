The Shoulders of Giants

Every year, I produce a big presentation digging into macro and strategic trends in the tech
industry. This year, 'Standing on the shoulders of giants' looks at what it means that 4bn
people have a smartphone; we connected everyone, and now we wonder what the Next Big
Thins is, but meanwhile, connecting everyone means we connected all the problems. Tech is
becoming a regulated industry, but we don't really know what that will mean.
