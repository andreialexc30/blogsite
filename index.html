The end of the American internet
For its first two decades, the consumer internet was American -
American companies, products, attitudes and laws set the
agenda. That’s not so true anymore - there are more smartphones
in China than in the USA and Western Europe
combined. Software creation and company creation is diffusing,
and attitudes are fragmenting. 3 Oct 2020
Amazon's profits, AWS and advertising
The bigger Amazon gets, the more it’s worth reading the
accounts. Does AWS subsidise the whole thing? Is the revenue
$250bn - or $450bn? And is that ad business just a footnote, or
is it bringing in more cash than AWS? 6 Sep 2020
The ecommerce surge
Both the UK and (today) the USA have given official statistics on
how ecommerce and retail have changed during lockdown. The
headline numbers are pretty dramatic. The UK went from 20%
ecommerce penetration to over 30% in two months, and the
USA from 17% to 22%. 18 Aug 2020
